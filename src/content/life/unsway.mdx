## AI Internship at Unsway

This was my first internship.

I worked as an AI intern at Unsway, which is a student led startup. Most of my work was around helping build an AI course.

I contributed by working on parts of the curriculum, writing some tutorials, and helping with lecture content  it was mostly just applying whatever I knew at that point and trying to explain things in a simple way.

I also completed the AI curriculum we were building, which helped me understand the basics a bit more clearly.

I did get “Employee of the Month” during this which was nice and I also got to work with a bunch of really cool teenagers which made the whole thing more fun

It wasn't anything too big, but it was my first time working on something like this, so yeah, it mattered to me :D
