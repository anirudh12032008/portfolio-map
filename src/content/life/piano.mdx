## Why this matters to me
I started learning piano from 2016 and as a kid I was very curious to learn new things and since then Piano has become a part of my life I even started to make videos about piano covers and sharing piano tutorials. 

I also performed in many events through my school and I have even performed in a few family weddings ( unfortunately I don't have any videos of the weddings but I have some videos of my school performances )

Playing piano is not just a hobby for me, its a way to feel myself and to create a small music would which makes me fell in love with it more and more every single day :)
