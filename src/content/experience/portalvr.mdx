## Why this matters to me

The idea started when I came across A frame.

I was always fascinated by VR and AR related stuff and as VR was getting to become more popular I decided to make a VR experience on my own and I created a cardboard VR experience also to try it out and see how it works and then when I saw Hackclub slack and searched for VR related projects I was shocked that there is no such project and I was like "wait a sec why not create one so that other can also try this suppperr cool worlds" I realized that while there were many programs in Hack Club, there hadn't been one focused on VR. I didn't have prior experience organizing something at that scale, but I thought it would be worth trying.

the thought struck my mind and I started to make a draft for the YSWS idea, only people connected somehow to HQ were mostly making their YSWS events so at that tieme so I was like oh no it would not work or something but I still drafted the Idea, Created a small demo website and created message and made the RSVP form

and I got 44 RSVPs and I was like "yipeeeeee" people actually want to try this and I was genuienly sooo happy to see that people are interested in trying out this project! Then I tried to pitch it to find sponsers it took a while but then finally Dhamari came up to me and asked to know more about the project and he loved the idea and was very happy to sponser it and be my POC for this project!
once this was final I started to work on the:
Program design: defining what participants would build and how they would get started ( in the end I decided to make it a 3 tier based program depending on the complexity and time spent)
