## Why this matters to me
I was just sitting in a normal Computer class in school in 2019 when I saw the last page of our textbook! and woohh it was different then the old MS paint, Word, Powerpoint! it was something completely new to me, it was about a programming language called Qbase, as I was always very curious about new stuff I started to read it and installed it locally on the machine but I didn't knew anything nor the information was enough to make something but there was a site link which had the information about loops, if else, inputs, print commands.

trying to experiment with it I tried a few lines and the thing that amazed me was that I wrote something and hit a button and whattt it actually works instantly ^^ it was like magic to me and I was like "wooohh I can make something with this????" and I started to try a bit more and I was able to create my first program!! it was 47 lines of code which I then worked more to increase to like about 200-300 to make it like a full game and it was a simple quiz game called KBC ( Kaun Banega Crorepati ) where you have to answer the questions and after each correct answer you get to the next tier and you balance increases and after wrong you go back to the lower tier

and I hit play and it actually worked!!!!!!! I can't tell in words how happy I was this was my first program and I never knew I could create something on my own :) since that day I started to learn more about computers, programming and even started game development right after this! 

PS - I also got into the In Real Life Kaun Banega Crorepati in 2023 and the younger me would have been soo proud of me to see that he actually got into the Game that he created in his childhood :D
