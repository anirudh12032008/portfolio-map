## What happened

I built a simple quiz app in QBASIC and presented it at our school science fair.
It was one of the first times I saw software become something people could interact with directly.

## Why it mattered

That project changed coding from a hobby into a path.
It made me want to keep building, sharing, and improving through real feedback.
